# Structural Alignment and Ligand Interaction of Human TPI1

## Project Overview

This project investigates the three-dimensional structure of human TPI1 using PyMOL. Structural alignment was performed between human and rabbit TPI1 proteins, followed by visualization of the interaction between the human enzyme and one of its ligands.

## Objectives

- Perform structural alignment between human and rabbit TPI1 proteins.
- Visualize ligand binding to human TPI1.
- Explore structural conservation between homologous proteins.

## Tools Used

- PyMOL
- Protein Data Bank (PDB)
